About Terry Davis

Terry A. Davis (1969–2018) was a programmer best known for creating the TempleOS operating system entirely by himself. He was known for his unique vision and eccentric personality.

Major Projects

  • TempleOS – A biblical-themed 64-bit operating system
  • Numerous custom tools and compilers built by hand
